Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using Spendly. This includes your account registration details (such as your name and email address) and the financial log transactions you input (including categories, descriptions, dates, and currency amounts of your expenses).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services of Spendly. Specifically, we use it to calculate your monthly spending breakdowns, categorize your expenses, and display personalized financial statistics. We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal financial records to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account details and expense records are stored securely in our database. We employ standard technical and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your financial information with third-party service providers except as required to maintain the hosting and technical infrastructure of our application. All third-party providers are obligated to protect your data with comparable privacy standards.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@spendly.io.
